Chapati Recipe

Chapati is a simple and nutritious flatbread that is easy to prepare at home.It is enjoyed by many families and can be served with a variety of dishes such as beans,stew,vegetables and meat.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of wheat flour
  • 1 Cup of warm water
  • 2 Tablespoons of cooking oil
  • 1/2 Teaspoons salt
  • Extra flour for dusting

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour and salt in bowl.
  2. Add cooking oil and mix well.
  3. Gradually add warm water and knead to form a sott dough.
  4. Cover the dough and let it to rest for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Divide the dough into small balls.
  6. Roll each ball into a flat round shape.
  7. Heat a pan and cook each chapati until golden brown on both sides.
